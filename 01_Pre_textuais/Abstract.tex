\begin{center}
\textbf{ABSTRACT}
\end{center}

$\!$\\
\noindent
This work presents the development of an analytical pipeline integrated with a decision-support dashboard for the management of the technological assets of the Hospital das Clinicas of the Federal University of Pernambuco. The study addresses resource allocation under budget constraints, focusing on the replacement of obsolete medical equipment. The proposed methodology includes the automation of extraction, transformation, and loading stages to clean and consolidate operational data, followed by the definition of a failure-criticality prioritization index. Based on this index, a multicriteria optimization model is proposed to recommend which damaged assets should be replaced first, maximizing the impact of the available budget. The expected contribution is to provide the Clinical Engineering department with an analytical tool to support strategic resource allocation and improve the recovery of clinical service capacity.

\hspace{-1.3cm}Keywords: Clinical Engineering, ETL, Multicriteria optimization, Resource allocation, Medical equipment management.
